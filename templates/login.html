NFTStache







What is NFTStache?
NFTStache is your introduction into the world of NFTs.
Our app will teach you everything you need to know about NFTs, ranging from
what they exactly are, to being able to know how to actually purchase them.
Explore real NFTs that are currently on the marketplace, and learn about their real time details.

Our Goal
The topic of NFTs has been on the rise recently and has been booming in popularity.
With them being so popular and novel, this leaves many people curious about the world of NFTs.
Our goal is to get people on the NFT hype train. We want to provide the knowledge so people can
know everything that they need to know about NFTs. Since the world of NFTs can be very lucrative,
and its an emerging technology, we want people to be informed on this new phenomenon that is taking over.
