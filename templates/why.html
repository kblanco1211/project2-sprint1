Why are NFTs valuable

Well, NFTs are valuable because it is something that is non-fungible meaning it has unique properties so it can't be interchanged with something else. For example it could be a house, or a painting, which is one of a kind. In conetxt to show how uniquw they are you can take a photo of the painting or buy a print, but there will only ever be one original painting. Which leads me to say NFTs are "one-of-a-kind" assets in the digital world that can be bought and sold like any other piece of property, but which have no tangible form of their own. These digital tokens can be thought of as certificates of ownership for virtual or physical assets. Now a days NFTs are becoming more and more popular making people very wealthy. If you don't hop on the NFT wave and join later you might be left behind . NFTs are basically the new bitcoin.
